Modeling

This series captures Rito as both subject and storyteller, where his body, persona and emotional expression become mediums of art. Known for shaping narratives across various forms of media, here he steps into the frame, exploring identity and presence from another's perspective. Through these images taken by different photographers, Rito conveys his vision not just through the eye of the camera, but through the gaze of the observer, embodying the narrative and inviting viewers to experience his multifaceted approach to self-expression.
